## Overview

**Optimization Grand Challenge 2024 (OGC 2024)** is an algorithm competition focused on solving real-world delivery optimization problems.

### What is Delivery Optimization?

When orders come through a food delivery app, multiple orders must be bundled and assigned to riders with optimal routes. This must satisfy the following constraints:

- Each order can be picked up after its ready time
- All orders must be delivered before their deadline
- Rider capacity limits must be respected
- Travel time and delivery costs must be minimized
