## Final Presentation Preparation

All final-stage teams must prepare for presentation evaluation on September 19.

Presentation evaluation is conducted as **5 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A** per team.

Teams must prepare a 5-minute presentation video in advance. (**At the venue, the pre-submitted video is played and then Q&A proceeds.**)

The 5-minute presentation video should freely include the following:

- **Algorithm logic**: overall structure and details of the algorithm
- **Algorithm implementation**: implementation language, packages used, techniques
- **Algorithm strengths**: what the team wants to emphasize (e.g., creativity, applicability, scalability)
- **Future development direction**: future improvements and methods not used due to constraints
- **Competition participation review**: impressions, suggestions, and future directions for the challenge
